# Creator Platform App 🚀

Welcome to the ultimate command center for Creator-Educators and an undeniably premium learning experience for professional students.

## 🎉 Latest Release Updates (v2.0.0)

We've just shipped our massive v2 update packed with core AI-driven tools designed to elevate the student learning experience and streamline content ingestion for educators.

### 🎓 Learner Features (Professional Upskilling)

* **Contextual Note-Taking (Save Insight):** A one-tap "Save Insight" button on the video player that instantly queries the active video segment, understands exactly what the teacher is saying, and generates a beautifully Markdown-formatted AI study note directly into your notebook.
* **Just-in-Time Video Stitching:** An intelligent semantic search pipeline built with Genkit. The backend automatically segments long-form videos into contiguous conceptual blocks. Students can search for a concept, and the app will stitch together relevant micro-clips from all unlocked courses into a custom playlist.
* **Drop-to-Audio ("Commute Mode"):** Switch to a low-bandwidth, audio-only stream for listening on the go, maximizing learning efficiency without draining mobile data.

### 👨‍🏫 Creator Command Center & Ingestion

* **Robust YouTube Import Engine:** A fully client-side YouTube video fetching system (bypassing cloud IP blocks) that allows educators to seamlessly download videos from their channel and sync them directly to their Firebase Storage bucket.

## 🌟 Previous Features (v1.0.0)

### 🤖 The YouTube AutoFunnel (Comment Management)

* **Secure YouTube Integration:** Connect your YouTube channel directly from your Teacher Dashboard securely via Google OAuth.
* **On-Demand Comment Syncing:** Fetch the latest comments from your most recent YouTube videos with a single tap.
* **Context-Aware AI Analysis:** Our AI reads every comment and understands whether it's a generic statement or a high-intent question from a potential student.
* **Instant AI Reply Generation:** Automatically draft highly contextual, professional replies to student doubts using the power of Gemini.
* **Seamless Course Plugging:** The AI seamlessly acts as your assistant, answering the doubt while naturally plugging a link to your premium course.
* **In-App Publishing:** Review, edit, and post your replies directly to the YouTube comment thread without ever leaving the app.

### 👨‍🏫 Creator Command Center

* **Beautiful Teacher Dashboard:** A completely revamped, intuitive dashboard giving you a bird's-eye view of your students, revenue, and content.
* **Streamlined Content Management:** Easily upload, edit, hide, or preview your courses and video materials.
* **Integrated Speed Dial:** A floating action button that gives you instant access to your most important tools: Uploading, Polls, Live Attempts, and the YouTube AutoFunnel.

## 🗺️ Product Roadmap (v3.0.0 & Beyond)

The project has reached a major milestone and development is paused for 2 months. When we return, we will be tackling the next generation of features to build a 10x experience:

### 🚀 Upcoming Learner Features

* **Chat with Video:** Expand upon the `student_notes` collection by allowing students to open a conversational interface and ask follow-up questions directly to the AI about the video they are watching.
* **Student Gamification:** Implement XP, learning streaks, and achievement badges to get students addicted to learning and dramatically increase course completion rates.
* **Roleplay Simulator Mode:** An AI chat/voice roleplay where students practice their skills against a simulated real-world stakeholder to earn rewards.
* **Speed-Up-In-Silence ("Smart Speed"):** AI automatically speeds up video playback during silences and filler words to save time.
* **Socratic Escrow Savings:** Ask the AI to "guide" you to an answer instead of giving it directly, saving your platform credits.

### 📈 Upcoming Educator Features

* **AI Video Engine (Marketing Assets):** Turn the current UI mockups into reality! The backend will analyze your video's transcript to find the most engaging 60 seconds, auto-crop it vertically, and generate downloadable Shorts & Reels for maximum top-of-funnel reach.
* **Teacher Heatmaps:** Aggregate data on exactly *where* students are pausing and clicking "Save Insight", surfacing a heatmap on the Teacher Dashboard to highlight the most valuable parts of your lectures.
* **Global Auto-Dubbing:** Automatically translate, dub, and lip-sync your English courses into multiple languages to 10x your addressable market.
* **Ghost-Course Predictor:** AI analyzes student doubts across all your courses to automatically generate a data-backed outline for your *next* highly-profitable course.
