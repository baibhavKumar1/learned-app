# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 4, 2026

*Disclaimer: This document is a template for informational purposes. Consult with a qualified attorney to ensure compliance with all applicable laws and regulations specific to your situation.*

Welcome to the **Learned App** (the "App", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a secure experience while using our application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use our services as a Student or a Teacher.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect depend on how you use our App.

### A. Information You Provide to Us
* **Account Information:** When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials (managed securely via Firebase Authentication). We may also collect profile information such as a profile picture if you sign up using Google Sign-In.
* **Student-Specific Data:** Subject preferences (e.g., Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology), board details, grade levels, quiz and chapter test scores, progress logs, XP (Experience Points), and submitted content explanations.
* **Teacher-Specific Data:** Academic qualification certificates, professional credentials, verification details, demo videos uploaded during the verification process, and bank/payout details (for earnings withdrawal).
* **Communication Data:** Direct messages, chat logs between students and teachers, community doubts, questions posted publicly, and queries submitted to our AI Doubt Assistant.
* **Transaction Metadata:** Purchase selections (plans, subscriptions, credit packages), transactions, and wallet balances under our Credit Escrow Economy. We do **not** collect or store credit card details; all payments are processed securely by third-party payment gateways (e.g., Stripe, Razorpay).

### B. Information Automatically Collected
* **Usage Details:** Information about your interactions with the App, including videos watched, content downloads, time spent on chapters, and button clicks.
* **Device Information:** Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and basic network information.
* **Cached AI Data:** Student queries submitted to the AI Doubt Assistant may be cached and analyzed to improve the assistant's performance and facilitate query recycling for the platform's economy.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:
1. **Provide and Maintain Services:** To route you to the correct portal (Student or Teacher Dashboard), display custom course feeds, stream lecture videos, and enable chat communication.
2. **Verification and Security:** To verify teacher qualifications and review demo videos before granting teacher credentials.
3. **Credit Escrow System:** To manage splits (royalty splits, platform fees, and completion rewards) and process payout withdrawals for teachers.
4. **AI-Driven Doubt Assistant:** To answer student queries, grade student explanations, and run the cached query lookup database to reduce server API overhead.
5. **Analytics and Support:** To track user progression (XP), debug errors, and optimize video streaming (HLS chunking and quality switching).

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share your information only with service providers who help us run the App:
* **Infrastructure & Storage:** Google Cloud Platform (GCS, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Transcoder API) for hosting data and processing videos.
* **Authentication:** Firebase Auth to manage secure logins.
* **Payment Processing:** Stripe or Razorpay to manage subscriptions, credit purchases, and withdrawals.
* **AI Processing:** Third-party AI model providers (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini) to power the Doubt Assistant and automated grading (queries sent are stripped of PII where possible).

---

## 4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The App is designed for students, some of whom may be under the age of 13. 
* We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
* We require parental consent before collecting personal information from students under 13 years of age.
* Parents can review their child's data, request deletion, or refuse further collection by contacting us.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

Under regulations like GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA, you have specific rights regarding your personal data:
* **Access and Portability:** You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
* **Correction:** You can update or correct your profile details directly inside the App settings.
* **Erasure (Deletion):** You have the right to request that we delete all your personal data. 

To request data deletion, please fill out our [Data Deletion Request Form](data_deletion_form.html) or email us at **support@learnedapp.com** with the subject "Data Deletion Request".

## 6. Security of Your Data

We use industry-standard security measures (SSL/TLS encryption, secure Cloud Firestore rules, Firebase Authentication tokens) to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
